## Open Questions

1. **119 unmatched leads**: Batch 2 had 256 scored but only 137 matched to dispo. The 119 unmatched may be from different buyers or time periods not in this dispo report. Getting dispos for those would strengthen validation.
2. **Renter conversion in roofing**: Only 1 renter appointment, no sale. Need more renter dispos to decide if renter should be stronger negative in roofing vs solar.
3. **Gold vs Silver boundary**: Gold converts at 13% vs Silver at 6.2% — the tier boundary appears correct. But need more data to validate the score cutoff (70 for Gold vs 45 for Silver).
4. **TrustedForm impact**: Batch 2 had no TrustedForm data. In production, form behavior signals would be available and may improve Bronze identification.
5. **Speed to contact matters**: The one contract was contacted in 1 minute. Should we add speed-to-contact guidance in buyer routing?
